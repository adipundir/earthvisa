## ⚠️ Data confidence & methodology (read first)

**These search volumes are ESTIMATES, not live tool exports.** They could not be pulled directly from Ahrefs/SEMrush in this session because those dashboards are JS apps behind login/captcha (can't be driven headlessly) and the Firecrawl browser-action quota was exhausted. The numbers below are derived from:

- **Verified demand signals** (web-searched): the SERPs for "visa free countries for indians" are saturated by aggregators (GoDigit, BankBazaar, VisaGuide, Airalo, IndiGo, Gulf News) → high commercial volume; the term spikes on policy news (Thailand waiver drove +26% Indian searches); "passport index" is brand-locked by Henley/Arton.
- **Category knowledge** of relative magnitudes in this niche.

**Confidence column:** `H` = well-known head term, estimate likely within ±30%. `M` = moderate. `L` = niche/long-tail, rough.

**To finalize (free):** paste each keyword into **Google Keyword Planner** (Tools → Keyword Planner → "Get search volume") with location = India and = Worldwide; or **Ahrefs Free Keyword Generator** / **SEMrush Keyword Overview**. India-locale terms must be checked with India geo, not US default. Replace the `Est. /mo` column with the tool's "Avg. monthly searches."

## 6. Recommended target priority

1. **Head capture (per-passport pages):** `visa free countries for [nationality]` - lead with India (~135k/mo), then Philippines/Nigeria/Pakistan/US. Title format: _"Visa-Free Countries for [Nationality] Passport Holders (2026)"_ + sections for VoA / eTA / e-Visa.
2. **Brand-adjacent:** a "passport power / index" comparison page to compete with Henley/Arton on long-tail ("compare [A] vs [B] passport").
3. **Own the wedge:** `countries you can visit with a US/Schengen visa`, `golden visa / citizenship by investment` pages - official-sourced, which incumbents lack.
4. **Tool intent:** a "do I need a visa? / where can I go" interactive checker page (matches `visa checker` intent and your product UX).
